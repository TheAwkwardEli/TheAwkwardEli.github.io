Animation

Rotoscope Composition

This piece is a collage of two different pieces of footage combined to make one cohesive animation. I wanted to use rotoscoping, a technique known for making realistic animations, to create something more fantastic and unique than what is usually created with that technique.

Terry Gilliam Tribute Animation

For this longer form animation, we were tasked with making a digital animation in the style of traditional animator and member of Monty Python, Terry Gilliam. I wanted to take more than the style of animation into account, and tried to add tension breaking humor.

Gravity and Bounce Demonstration

Using different styles of digital animation, I created balls of different sizes and different speeds bouncing around the screen. This was a demonstration of every type of modern animation.

Illustration and Design

Microplastic Infographic

This infographic was made to spread awareness of the true nature of microplastics. Many target primary microplastics as the main problem in the ocean, but secondary microplastics are far more threatening. This infographic goes through the creation of these secondary microplastics specifically.

Lightning Knight Reference Sheet

This rough character reference was built to practice my quick character making skills. I gave myself two hours to make a character concept and draw it. I created a tired veteran knight who has the power of lightning on his side. This reference sheet is used for a dungeons and dragons campaign.

Moonfish

This Opah Moonfish illustration was drawn in a series of pieces representing the unique creatures of the world. The Opah Moonfish was chosen because it is the only warm blooded fish.
