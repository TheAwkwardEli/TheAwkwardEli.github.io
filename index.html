Interests

Photography and Videography

My interest in photography inspires all of my other work. My illustrations and animations mimic the camera work that I comes from photographers and cinemetographers that I admire. These influences make all of my other work more dynamic and weighted.

Illustration

My interest in animals and biology inspires my illustrations. I appreciate the anatomy and behaviours of real life creatures, bringing them into my own creature and character design. My illustrations tend to include nature in some form. Be it actual plants and animals or the bits of random wear and tear in a cityscape.

Animation

Animation has always fascinated me. These past couple years, I have focused more and more on learning the processes behind animation and practicing as much as possible. I admire the creativity and freedom of 2d animation, and I admire the consistency of 3d animation.
